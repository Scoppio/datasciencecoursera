# CodeBook

## Datasets

Three datasets are present, all of them have the same variables.
 *  full_data.csv: Test and train data stacked as a single dataframe
 *  mean_data.csv: Dataframe with only means of the columns
 *  std_data.csv: Dataframe with only standard deviation of the columns
